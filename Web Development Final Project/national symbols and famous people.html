National Symbols and Famous People

In this portion of the website you will all you need to know on the National Symbols and the famous people of this rich and beautiful country.

National Symbols

One of the first national symbols that most people know of is the Italian Flag. The Italian flag is recognized all around the globe. Another symbol that most likely less people know of is the emblem of Italy which represents the Italian Republic. The Italian National anthem is the third most well-known National Symbol of that country. There is also the Festa della Repubblica, that is the national celebratory day established to commemorate the birth of the Italian Republic, which is celebrated every year on 2 June, date of the institutional referendum of 1946 with which the monarchy was abolished. For an animal that repersents Italy, there is the Italian wolf, which inhabits Apennine Mountains and the Western Alps, features prominently in Latin and Italian cultures, such as in the legend of the founding of Rome. It is unofficially considered the national animal of Italy. Finally there is the strawberry tree, or the small tree chosen as a national tree because of its green leaves, its white flowers and its red berries, colors that recall the Italian flag; The flower of the strawberry tree is the national flower of Italy.
